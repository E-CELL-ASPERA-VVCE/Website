About Us

ASPERA is a dynamic student-driven initiative at Vidyavardhaka College of Engineering (VVCE), dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship, innovation, and leadership skills. Through various workshops, events, and collaborative opportunities, ASPERA empowers students to turn their ideas into impactful ventures.

Vision

To foster entrepreneurship, innovation, and startup culture in both students and faculty.

Mission

To empower students with the knowledge, resources, and mentorship needed to transform their ideas into thriving ventures.

Tagline

Actuating Ambition

Values

  • Innovation: Host workshops on brainstorming techniques, design thinking, and prototyping.
  • Collaboration: Organize team-building activities and workshops.
  • Inclusivity: Provide mentoring and support to students from all backgrounds, including underrepresented groups.
  • Impact: Invite successful entrepreneurs to share their stories and offer advice to students.
  • Empowerment: Give students and faculty the opportunity to lead their own projects and initiatives.
  • Transformation: Help students and faculty to develop business plans and to launch their startups.
